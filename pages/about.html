Our Story

Founded in 2015, Handyman Services began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality handyman services to homeowners in our local community. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of skilled professionals dedicated to solving your home repair and improvement needs.

Our founder, Mike Johnson, spent over 20 years working in various trades before deciding to start his own handyman business. Frustrated by the lack of reliable, honest service providers in the area, he set out to create a company that homeowners could truly trust.

Today, we're proud to be the go-to handyman service for hundreds of satisfied customers. We've built our reputation on quality workmanship, fair pricing, and exceptional customer service. No job is too small, and we approach each project with the same level of care and attention to detail.
