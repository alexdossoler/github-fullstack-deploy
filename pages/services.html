Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the entire local area and surrounding communities within a 30-mile radius. If you're unsure whether we service your area, please contact us, and we'll be happy to let you know.

How do I schedule a service?

You can schedule a service by calling us directly at (864) 743-3178 or by filling out the contact form on our website. We'll get back to you promptly to confirm your appointment.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all our services. You can request an estimate by phone or through our website. For more complex jobs, we may need to schedule an in-person visit to provide an accurate quote.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. Payment is typically due upon completion of the service, unless other arrangements have been made in advance.

Are your handymen licensed and insured?

Yes, all our handymen are licensed, insured, and have undergone thorough background checks. We take pride in our professional team and ensure they have the necessary qualifications to provide high-quality service.

Do you offer any guarantees on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, we'll make it right at no additional cost to you. Additionally, we offer a 90-day warranty on all labor.
